about

I’ve been a professional writer for more than 25 years, helping dozens of businesses with a wide variety of marketing communications material. My feature articles and advertorials have appeared online and in regional and national publications. Born and raised in Houston, I’ve also lived in Dallas, Austin, New York City and Los Angeles.


As a natural outgrowth of my interest in travel writing, several years ago I began organizing week-long vacations in Tuscany for family, friends and friends of friends. I chronicle many of my experiences there in my Italian travel blog, Addicted to Italy. I’m also publishing a book about the sources, processes and details of planning a group trip to Tuscany. It’s called Ciao, Sweetie! How to Vacation in a Tuscan Farmhouse. That book, as well as a short crime fiction novel I wrote in 2019 called Dangerous Practice, are available through Amazon under my pen name, Suzanne Waltz.
